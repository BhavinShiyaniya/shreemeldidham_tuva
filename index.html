About Temple

Nestled in the serene village of Tuva, Surendranagar, Gujarat, Shree Meldidham is a sacred pilgrimage site dedicated to Maa Meldi, the divine goddess of protection, strength, and devotion. This holy temple stands as a beacon of faith, attracting devotees from across India who seek blessings, peace, and spiritual upliftment.

About Gaushala

At Shree Meldidham Gau Shala, we are deeply committed to the protection, care, and well-being of cows, which are revered as sacred in Hindu Dharma. Located within the divine premises of Shree Meldidham Temple in Tuva, Surendranagar, our Gau Shala serves as a sanctuary for cows, reflecting the principles of Gau Seva (service to cows) and Ahimsa (non-violence).
