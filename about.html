About Shree Meldidham

Shree Meldidham, located in Tuva, Surendranagar, Gujarat, India, is a sacred temple dedicated to Maa Meldi. This divine temple has been a beacon of spirituality, attracting thousands of devotees seeking blessings, peace, and prosperity.


The temple is renowned for its traditional pujas, spiritual discourses, and grand festivals celebrating the glory of Maa Meldi. Devotees from across India visit this temple to offer prayers and seek divine guidance.


At Shree Meldidham, we honor ancient Hindu traditions, conducting daily aartis, bhajans, and religious ceremonies that fill the air with divine energy.
The temple is a center of faith, culture, and service, promoting values of dharma, seva (service), and Gau Raksha (cow protection). Our Gau Shala (cow sanctuary) is dedicated to protecting and nurturing cows, an essential part of our Sanatan Dharma.


Beyond its religious significance, Shree Meldidham is a symbol of unity and devotion, bringing people together through grand festivals, spiritual discourses, and social welfare activities. Every year, thousands of devotees visit during the Navratri Mahotsav, Meldi Mata Jayanti, and special pujas, experiencing the divine blessings of Maa Meldi.
